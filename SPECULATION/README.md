## Key Questions Answered

### Why does energy have multiple definitions?

### Why 10^123 vacuum energy problem?
**QFT and GR are both effective; their "vacuum energies" are artifacts using different coarse-grainings.**

### Why does geometry underdetermine physics?
**Geometry is coarse-grained description; quantum state contains additional information not captured by g_μν.**

### Why are observers important?
**Observer factorization is part of emergence procedure; different factorizations → different effective physics.**

### Why is time relational?
**Time emerges from correlations in timeless quantum state; depends on clock choice and observer.**

### Why are global theorems conditional? **[NEW]**
**Global properties emerge from coarse-graining; conditions specify domain where coarse-graining is valid.**

### Why does EFT break down smoothly? **[NEW]**
**Both GR and QFT are effective; breakdown happens when coarse-graining assumptions fail, not at fixed scale.**
