## The Bottom Line for Physics

### What Changes

**Research focus shifts from:**
- Quantizing gravity → Understanding spacetime emergence
- Finding UV completion → Finding pre-geometric substrate
- Resolving singularities → Explaining effective description breakdown
- Fixing energy definitions → Understanding energy emergence
- Maintaining observer-independence → Incorporating observers into quantum state

**Key paradigm shifts:**

1. **Spacetime is not fundamental arena**
   - It's emergent effective description
   - Valid only in appropriate coarse-graining limits
   - Observers are part of it, not external to it

2. **Energy is not fundamental conserved charge**
   - It's emergent statistical property
   - Multiple definitions reflect different coarse-grainings
   - Vacuum energy problem dissolves

3. **Quantum fields are not fundamental DOFs**
   - They emerge along with spacetime
   - Particle content depends on coarse-graining procedure
   - QFT-GR mismatch expected (both effective)
