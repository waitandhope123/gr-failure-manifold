# Confidence Assessment: Evidence Strength and Uncertainty

## Overall Confidence in Interpretations

**After 85 toys (13 phases):**

**Core claim:** Both GR and QFT are effective theories. Spacetime, quantum fields, observers, time, and causality emerge from pre-geometric quantum information.

**Sub-interpretations:**

#### A1: Fields on Fixed Spacetime (~25%, down from 35%)
- QFT fundamental, spacetime fixed background
- GR emerges from quantum field fluctuations

**Problems:**
- Toy 72: Cannot resolve 10^123 vacuum energy problem
- Toy 82: EFT breakdown inevitable, shows GR is explicitly effective
- Toys 74-75, 80: Cannot explain observer ontology issues
- Doesn't explain why spacetime itself seems observer-dependent

#### A2: Pre-Geometric Quantum Information (~55-60%, up from 45-50%)
- Neither geometry nor fields fundamental
- Both emerge from quantum information substrate
- Observers, time, causality also emergent

**Strengths:**
- Explains all failure modes coherently
- Consistent with holography, ER=EPR
- Supported by Phase XIII (conditional theorems, contextual EFT)
- Matches historical thermodynamics → statistical mechanics transition

---

### Interpretation B: Corrections (~15-20%)

**Core claim:** GR's geometric framework correct, but equations need modification (modified gravity, etc.).

**Problems:**
- Cannot explain information-theoretic evidence (Phase XI)
- Cannot explain observer ontology breakdown (Phase XII)

- Still faces vacuum energy catastrophe
- Doesn't explain systematic emergence patterns
